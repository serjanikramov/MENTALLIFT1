Promoting Awareness and Support for Mental Health

Why Mental Health Matters

Mental health is just as important as physical health. It affects how we think, feel, and act. Good mental health helps us cope with stress, relate to others, and make healthy decisions.

Common Issues

  • Depression
  • Anxiety
  • Burnout
  • Low Self-Esteem

What You Can Do

Talk to someone you trust. Reach out to mental health professionals. Practice self-care, get enough sleep, eat healthy, and exercise regularly.
